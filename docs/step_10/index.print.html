_index.en

D4_DU_prevalence_MS_in_pregnancy_cohort

contains the aggregated prevalence (numerators and denominators) needed to compute prevalence of MS in the pregnancy cohort, to feed Template 4 and 5

D4_DU_individual_prevalence_of_use_MSmeds_in_MSpregnancy_trimesters

this dataset measures in each MS pregnancy whether the woman used or didn't use each of the MS medications in the trimesters before or during pregnancy (to ultimately feed Template 3)

D4_DU_prevalence_of_exclusive_use_MSmeds_in_MSpregnancy_trimesters

this is the aggregation of the individual-level dataset D4_DU_individual_prevalence_of_use_MSmeds_in_MSpregnancy_trimesters, to feed Template 3. It copntains counts of persons using medicines exclusively in some periods before and during pregnancy

D4_DU_prevalence_of_use_MSdrugs_in_MS_cohort

contains the aggregated numerators needed to compute prevalence of use of MS medications in the MS cohort, to feed Template 6 and 7.
